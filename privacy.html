Privacy Notice

Effective July 23, 2026

This notice describes the information used by the current MMS Connect account, program-guidance, and Referral Lite portal. This release does not accept Medicaid applications, Social Security numbers, medical records, financial documents, client contact details, case narratives, or document uploads.

Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, selected account type, and—when applicable—your agency or facility name. Our authentication provider processes account credentials and verification information. Referral Lite stores the sending and receiving organizations, a broad service category, urgency, consent-confirmation time, random referral reference, status, status times, and the account responsible for each status action.

How we use information

  • Provide and secure your account.
  • Verify agency and facility registrations.
  • Route anonymous service referrals and show their status.
  • Show role-appropriate program guidance and official application links.
  • Prevent misuse and maintain operational security.

What not to submit

Referral Lite does not provide a field for a client name, contact information, date of birth, identifier, medical or financial information, free-text summary, note, intake record, or document. Do not place that information in account or organization fields or ordinary email. Coordinate identifying information outside MMS Connect through an approved secure channel. Use NC ePASS or your county Department of Social Services for an official application.

Service providers and external links

MMS Connect uses service providers for hosting and authentication. Official NCDHHS, DSS, and ePASS links open services governed by their own privacy notices.

Your choices

You may request account assistance or account deletion by contacting admin@medicaid-made-simple.com or 252-286-1033. Records may be retained when reasonably necessary for security, legal, or operational obligations.

Changes

We may update this notice as the platform changes. Material changes will be reflected by a new effective date.
