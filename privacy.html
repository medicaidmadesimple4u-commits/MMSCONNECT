Privacy Notice

Effective July 16, 2026

This notice describes the information used by the current MMS Connect account and program-guidance portal. This release does not accept Medicaid applications, Social Security numbers, medical records, financial documents, or document uploads.

Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, selected account type, and—when applicable—your agency or facility name. Our authentication provider processes account credentials and verification information.

How we use information

  • Provide and secure your account.
  • Verify agency and facility registrations.
  • Show role-appropriate program guidance and official application links.
  • Prevent misuse and maintain operational security.

What not to submit

Do not send confidential Medicaid, medical, financial, immigration, or identity information through account fields or ordinary email. Use NC ePASS or your county Department of Social Services for an official application.

Service providers and external links

MMS Connect uses service providers for hosting and authentication. Official NCDHHS, DSS, and ePASS links open services governed by their own privacy notices.

Your choices

You may request account assistance or account deletion by contacting admin@medicaid-made-simple.com or 252-286-1033. Records may be retained when reasonably necessary for security, legal, or operational obligations.

Changes

We may update this notice as the platform changes. Material changes will be reflected by a new effective date.
